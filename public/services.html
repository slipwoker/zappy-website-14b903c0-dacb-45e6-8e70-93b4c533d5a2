Luxury Design Services by Shadazzle Strawberry Diamond Creations™

Choose one custom service or bundle multiple services together for a complete luxury brand look. Every design is created to shine online, in print, and in person.

Our Signature Design Services

Every design we create combines luxury style with professional precision. From custom stickers that sparkle to powerful brand logos, we craft designs that help businesses stand out and shine across every platform.

Sticker & Decal Design 

Eye-catching custom stickers and decals designed to make your brand stand out. Perfect for product packaging, laptops, bottles, promotional campaigns, and merchandise. Each design combines bold visuals with luxury finishes that bring your brand to life.

Deliverables: Print-ready vector files, multiple size options (2"-6"), finish recommendations (glossy, matte, holographic), die-cut specifications, and placement guidance for maximum visibility. 

Brand Marks & Monograms

Distinctive logo creators and signature monograms that capture your essence in a single mark. Whether you need a full brand identity system or an elegant personal monogram, we craft symbols that resonate with sophistication and remain memorable across all applications.

Deliverables:Primary logo suite, monogram variations, color and black-and-white versions, minimum size specifications, clear space guidelines, and usage documentation for consistent brand expression.

Product Labels & Packaging Design

Luxury label and packaging designs that turn your product into a premium experience. From cosmetics and candles to food products and custom merchandise, we design packaging that grabs attention, builds trust, and makes your brand unforgettable.

Deliverables:Production-ready artwork with bleed and trim marks, substrate recommendations, color-matching specifications (Pantone/CMYK), finishing effects (foil, emboss, spot UV), and dieline templates for manufacturing.

Social Media & Promotional Graphics

Eye-catching social media graphics and promotional visuals designed to elevate your brand online. From Instagram posts and story templates to promotional flyers and launch announcements, we create graphics that capture attention and keep your brand consistent across every platform.

Deliverables: Platform-optimized graphics (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X), promotional flyer designs, story templates, highlight covers, and brand-consistent graphic templates for future campaigns.
